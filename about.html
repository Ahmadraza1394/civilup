About Us

Ziad Abdul Rahman Ahmed Krishan Engineering Consulting Office was established in (2012) and has several branches in Medina, Makkah Al-Mukarramah, Al-Ahsa and Jeddah.

In a short time, the office distinguished between the engineering offices and gained the confidence of customers.

The office has accreditation from the Municipality of the Holy Capital, the Municipality of Medina, the Municipality of Jeddah, Al-Ahsa Municipality, the General Corporation for Water Desalination, the Taiba Company for Airport Services, the State Real Estate.

Vision

We seek to improve the concept of architectural and construction design in line with the civilizational and economic development of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia with our aspiration to achieve the goals of Vision 2030 and provide best services to our customers.

Goal

Our goal is to create sustainable buildings with high quality and attractive civilized design, with the assurance that we are walking according to an organized plan in order to be the best Option for all customers and to be one of the first offices in the field of design and consultancy.
